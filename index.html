The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in
renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to
enhance connectivity and access to services for
all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure
a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh is a nation of resilience and limitless potential. Once known for poverty and natural disasters, today we are a symbol of hope and innovation. Our people have shown that with determination and creativity, even the greatest challenges can be overcome. Through initiatives like microfinance and social business, we have empowered millions, especially women, to transform their lives. Poverty is not created by the poor; it is a failure of the system. Together, we can build a Bangladesh free from poverty and inequality—a nation where every person has the opportunity to thrive and succeed."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
